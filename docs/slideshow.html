My favorite pictures from the Hubble Telescope

Pillars of Creation

When I was young I remember looking at pictures from the Hubble Telescope with my brother and being fascinated by it. The colors were amazing and the shapes were surreal and unlike anything I'd ever seen before.

In this slideshow I hope to capture some of the wonder I felt when I was young and present that to you. Please enjoy these spectacular images from deep space!
